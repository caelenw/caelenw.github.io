Golden Retrievers

The Golden Retriever is a breed of dog that is very widely known around the world. They are used for much more than just loving pets, including being service dogs and police K9s. They are unique in their own way, from their fast-wagging tails to their high drive to fetch. These dogs were meant to run but are also great for cuddling up on the sofa for a movie.



Golden Retrievers can be trained to perform tasks and are used by thousands every day to do just that. They have a remarkably strong sense of smell, so if you're cooking, they're going to want a taste. They are great family dogs to have around kids and love to play dress-up with the little ones. It's no secret that the Golden Retriever holds a special place in so many people's hearts.
