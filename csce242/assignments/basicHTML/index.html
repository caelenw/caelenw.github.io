Golden Retriever

Golden Retriever are all loved by almost anyone who has the chance to meet one.
Golden Retrievers are cute, crazy, loving, and silly making them irrisistable. They
are very social, and thus making them very eager to please their loving owner. They
might be crazy at times but that is what makes everyone love them so much.


Golden Retrivers have some very interesting facts that most people do not know.
Did you know that Golden Retrievers as puppys have webbed feet, and thus making
them excellent swimmers. Goldens are know to have a excelent sense of smell,
making them the perfect dog for drug detection, service animal, and hunting
companion. If you have every had the chance to spend time with a Golden Retriever
you would understand why so many people have them world wide.
