CSCE 522 - Farkas 19
Rijndael
Symmetric, block cipher
Key size: 128, 192, or 256 bits
Block size: 128
 Processed as 4 groups of 4 bytes (state)
Operates on the entire block in every round
Number of rounds depending on key size:
 Key=128  9 rounds
 Key=192  11 rounds
 Key=256  13 rounds
